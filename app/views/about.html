About Us

Welcome to our companion weather app!

Weather top is a Web Application to accompany the Weather Top 5000, a modular weather station for amateur and professional weather enthusiasts.

Purchase of a Weather Top 5000 grants lifetime free access to this companion app, a place where users can record and upload weather data from your weather station to your personalised dashboard, free of clutter and ads.

Please note: WeatherTop data is supplied by our user community. Observations are made from a variety of sources and therefore quality and frequency of observations may vary. Data is not reviewed, corrected or moderated in any way.
